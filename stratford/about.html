About Stratford

Our Mission

Stratford House exists to provide a space where men can excel academically and spiritually. We believe in "Iron sharpens Iron" (Proverbs 27:17).

House Duties

Residents participate in a streamlined chore system that ensures the house stays clean without being a burden on study time. This instills a sense of ownership and responsibility.

FAQ

What are the room arrangements?

We offer doubles and singles, subject to availability and seniority.

Do I have to be Baptist to live here?

No, men from all Christian denominations are welcome.
