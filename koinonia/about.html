About Koinonia

Cooperative Living

As a cooperative, we share the responsibilities of maintaining our home. This keeps costs low and builds character. Every member has a weekly chore (cleaning, cooking assistant, etc.) and participates in house work days.

Meals

We have a dedicated cook who prepares dinner Monday through Thursday. Breakfast and lunch are self-serve with a fully stocked kitchen open 24/7. Friday dinners are often leftovers or group outings.

FAQ

Is there air conditioning?

Yes, all rooms and common areas are air-conditioned.

How close is it to campus?

We are located just off Lincoln Ave, a short walk or bus ride to the Quad.
