Welcome to Koinonia

Fellowship & Faith

Koinonia (κοινωνία) means fellowship. Our house is built on the foundation of brotherhood and shared faith. We strive to encourage one another in our walk with Christ while pursuing academic success at UIUC.

Living here means being part of a family. From late-night study sessions to house devotionals, you'll find a supportive community that lasts a lifetime.
