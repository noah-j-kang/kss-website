About Sutton

Community Life

Sutton House is more than just a place to sleep. It's a vibrant community of women who support each other through college. From movie nights to Bible studies, there's always something happening.

Cooperative Duties

We share the work of keeping our home beautiful. Duties are assigned on a rotating basis and take only a few hours a week. This cooperative model encourages stewardship and service.

FAQ

Is the kitchen available for use?

Yes, the kitchen is fully equipped and available for residents to cook their own meals.

Is laundry included?

Yes, laundry facilities are free for all residents.
